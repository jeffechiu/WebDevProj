Welcome!

The Braddock Region Chamber of Commerce (BRCC) is a newly created chamber that serves as a neighborhood hub of connection, resources, skill building, and partnership for the Braddock and surrounding communities. The BRCC is a membership organization that supports businesses, youth entrepreneurs, nonprofits, and beneficaries of justice centered economic development.

What We Offer

Benefits:
  • Access an extensive directory to find the perfect partner(s) for your needs
  • Learn from educational classes and live seminars
  • Get matched with mentors to get specific guidance for your project
  • Get personalized content tailored just for you
